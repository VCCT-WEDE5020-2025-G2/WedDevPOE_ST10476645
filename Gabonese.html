Atanga

The atanga a popular gabonese savory snack.

Ingredients:

  • Atangas
  • salt
  • chilli(optional if not to tate)
  • Water for boiling
  1. Wash your atangas
  2. Then boil some water as if making pasta
  3. When boiling enough set aside then put the atanga in the hot water
  4. While in the water the atanga should start to soften
  5. To serve, take the sotten atangas out of the water and season to tate.
  6. Enjoy your atangas!

Bouillon de poisson(Fish Soup)

Bouillon de poisson is a traditional Gabonese fish soup made with fresh fish, vegetables, and spices, often served with plaintain or manioc(cassava) and other types of cassava.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh fish (such as tilapia)
  • Onions, chopped
  • Tomatoes,chopped
  • Okra
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (such as bay leaves)

Steps:

  1. Clean and cut the fish into pieces. Season with salt and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add okra to the vegetables
  4. Add the cut pieces of fish and add around 2 cups of water
  5. Let it simmer until the fish flesh is tender and fragrant
  6. Serve hot with plaintain, manioc(cassava), or rice.
  7. Enjoy your bouillon!
