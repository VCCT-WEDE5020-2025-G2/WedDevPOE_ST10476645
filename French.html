Ratatouille

A traditional french dish of stewed vegetables, such as tomato, garlic,onions,courgette,aubergines,bell pepers and herbs.

Ingredients:
  • 1 large eggplants
  • 1 large Zucchini
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 large tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Wash and cut the eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and onion into 1/2-inch cubes. Mince the garlic and chop the tomatoes if using fresh ones.
  2. Cook the Eggplant: Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the eggplant cubes and cook for about 5-7 minutes until they start to soften. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Cook the Zucchini and Bell Peppers: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil. Add the zucchini and bell peppers, cooking for another 5-7 minutes until they begin to soften. Remove and set aside with the eggplant.
  4. Saut√© the Onions and Garlic: Add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil to the skillet. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Add Tomatoes and Herbs: Stir in the chopped tomatoes (or canned tomatoes) into the skillet with the onions and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes until the tomatoes start to break down. Add dried thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Combine All Vegetables: Return the cooked eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers to the skillet. Stir everything together to combine well. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until all vegetables are tender and flavors meld together.
  7. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt, pepper, or herbs if needed.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Ratatouille can be served hot or at room temperature as a main dish or side dish.
